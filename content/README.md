## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Considerations

### Legal & Ethical
- ‚úÖ **DO:** Cite specific WCAG guidelines and date of testing
- ‚úÖ **DO:** Focus on product improvement, not company shaming
- ‚úÖ **DO:** Acknowledge when companies fix issues
- ‚ùå **DON'T:** Make legal claims ("This is illegal")
- ‚ùå **DON'T:** Exaggerate severity for engagement
- ‚ùå **DON'T:** Test only once without verification

### Brand Alignment
All content should align with WCAG AI Platform's core messaging:
- **People First** - Center disabled users in the conversation
- **Solution-Oriented** - Show how to fix, not just what's broken
- **"Cyborg Shield"** - AI + Human verification approach
- **Justice, Not Charity** - Accessibility is a right, not a favor

See [WCAGAI_Masonic_Messaging.md](../WCAGAI_Masonic_Messaging.md) for complete brand voice guidelines.
