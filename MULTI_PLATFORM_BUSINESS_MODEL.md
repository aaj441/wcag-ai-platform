# Multi-Platform Business Model
## WCAG AI Platform - Three Revenue Stream Strategy

**Executive Summary**: Diversified revenue model combining B2B accessibility consulting, B2C health insurance, and creative music projects to reach $200K+ annual revenue.

---

## Overview

### Strategic Vision
Build a sustainable, diversified consulting business with three complementary revenue streams that leverage shared infrastructure (AI, CRM, automation) while serving different market segments.

### Core Philosophy
1. **B2B Anchor**: WCAG consulting provides stable, high-value revenue
2. **B2C Supplemental**: Insurance and music add recurring/passive income
3. **Shared Infrastructure**: One platform powers all three businesses
4. **Risk Diversification**: Economic downturns affect streams differently

---

## Revenue Stream #1: WCAG Accessibility Consulting (B2B)

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: 30M+ US businesses with websites
- **SAM (Serviceable Available)**: 5M small-medium businesses
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable)**: 50-100 clients/year realistically
- **Growth Driver**: Increasing ADA litigation (4,600+ lawsuits in 2023)

### Service Offerings & Pricing

#### Tier 1: Basic Audit ($2,500-$5,000)
**Deliverables**:
- Single-page accessibility scan (5-10 pages)
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance report
- Prioritized violation list
- Basic remediation recommendations
- 30-day email support

**Ideal Client**: Small businesses (1-10 employees), single-site owners  
**Delivery Time**: 3-5 business days  
**Profit Margin**: 80% ($2,000-$4,000 profit)

#### Tier 2: Comprehensive Audit ($5,000-$10,000)
**Deliverables**:
- Multi-page website audit (20-50 pages)
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA + AAA analysis
- Detailed technical remediation guide
- VPAT 2.4 documentation
- Executive summary presentation
- 60-day email support + 1 follow-up review

**Ideal Client**: Mid-size businesses (10-100 employees), e-commerce sites  
**Delivery Time**: 1-2 weeks  
**Profit Margin**: 75% ($3,750-$7,500 profit)

#### Tier 3: Enterprise Package ($10,000-$25,000)
**Deliverables**:
- Full site audit (unlimited pages)
- Mobile app accessibility review
- Custom remediation roadmap with timeline
- Developer training workshop (2 hours)
- VPAT + ACR documentation
- Quarterly re-scans (Year 1)
- Priority support + monthly check-ins

**Ideal Client**: Large enterprises (100+ employees), SaaS companies  
**Delivery Time**: 2-4 weeks  
**Profit Margin**: 70% ($7,000-$17,500 profit)

#### Tier 4: Retainer Services ($3,000-$7,500/month)
**Deliverables**:
- Ongoing accessibility monitoring
- Monthly compliance reports
- On-demand consulting (10-20 hours/month)
- Priority remediation support
- Staff training sessions
- Legal compliance documentation

**Ideal Client**: Heavily regulated industries (healthcare, finance, government)  
**Profit Margin**: 65% ($1,950-$4,875 profit/month)

## Revenue Stream #2: Health Insurance Sales (B2C)

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM**: 30M+ uninsured/underinsured Americans
- **Focus**: Medicare Advantage, ACA Marketplace, Supplemental policies
- **Trend**: Aging Baby Boomers (10,000/day turning 65)

### Business Model: Commission-Based

#### Product Types & Commissions
| Product | Avg Premium | Commission Rate | Avg Commission |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Medicare Advantage | $200/mo | 6% annual | $144/year |
| Medicare Supplement | $150/mo | 20% FYC* | $360 (first year) |
| ACA Marketplace | $400/mo | 5-10% annual | $240-$480/year |
| Final Expense | $50/mo | 100-120% FYC | $600-$720 (first year) |
| Dental/Vision | $75/mo | 15% annual | $135/year |

*FYC = First Year Commission

#### Realistic Sales Targets

**Year 1 (Part-Time, 5-10 hrs/week)**:
- Medicare Advantage: 20 clients × $144 = $2,880
- Medicare Supplement: 10 clients × $360 = $3,600
- ACA Marketplace: 5 clients × $360 = $1,800
- **Total**: $8,280 + renewals (Year 2+: $5,000-$8,000/year passive)

**Year 2 (Part-Time, 10-15 hrs/week)**:
- Medicare Advantage: 40 clients × $144 = $5,760
- Medicare Supplement: 20 clients × $360 = $7,200
- ACA Marketplace: 15 clients × $360 = $5,400
- Renewals from Year 1: $5,000
- **Total**: $23,360

### Lead Generation
- **Aged Leads**: $1-$5 per lead (budget $200-$500/month)
- **Facebook Ads**: $10-$30 per lead
- **Referrals**: Existing WCAG clients (HR departments)
- **Community Events**: Senior centers, churches, libraries

### Licensing & Compliance
- **Required**: State insurance license(s) ($200-$500)
- **Study Time**: 2-4 weeks (20-40 hours)
- **Exam Fee**: $50-$75 per state
- **E&O Insurance**: $500-$1,000/year (separate from WCAG E&O)
- **Continuing Education**: 8-24 hours/year ($100-$300)

### Time Investment
- Licensing/Training: 40 hours upfront
- Client Consultations: 1-2 hours per client
- Annual Reviews: 30 mins per existing client
- **Weekly**: 5-10 hours ongoing (part-time side income)

## Revenue Stream #3: Music Projects (B2C/B2B)

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM**: Independent musicians, podcasters, content creators
- **Niche**: Accessible music experiences + sync licensing
- **Differentiator**: AI-powered metadata, accessibility focus

### Business Models

#### Model A: Subscription SaaS ($49-$99/month)
**Product**: Music metadata management + accessibility tools

**Features**:
- AI-powered metadata generation
- Spotify/Apple Music integration
- Accessible player widgets (WCAG compliant)
- Licensing tracking dashboard
- Analytics and reporting

**Target Customers**:
- Independent artists
- Small labels
- Podcast producers
- Music libraries

**Revenue Projections**:
- Year 1: 20 subscribers × $49/mo = $11,760
- Year 2: 50 subscribers × $69/mo = $41,400
- Year 3: 100 subscribers × $69/mo = $82,800

#### Model B: Sync Licensing (15% Commission)
**Product**: Music placement in videos, ads, films

**Process**:
1. Curate library of pre-cleared tracks
2. Partner with Epidemic Sound, Artlist, AudioJungle
3. Earn 15-25% commission on placements
4. Focus: Accessible, inclusive music (underserved market)

**Revenue Projections**:
- Year 1: $5,000-$10,000 (building catalog)
- Year 2: $15,000-$30,000 (established presence)
- Year 3: $40,000-$60,000 (passive income stream)

#### Model C: Custom Scoring ($500-$5,000/project)
**Product**: Custom music for accessible video content

**Services**:
- Audio description-friendly scores
- Music for deaf/hard-of-hearing audiences (visual music)
- Custom jingles for accessible brands
- Podcast intros/outros

**Target**: 2-4 projects/year (side projects)  
**Revenue**: $2,000-$10,000/year

### Time Investment
- Initial Setup: 80-100 hours (building platform)
- Weekly Maintenance: 2-5 hours
- **Model**: Mostly passive after setup (software/licensing)

## Risk Mitigation

### Diversification Benefits
| Risk | Impact on WCAG | Impact on Insurance | Impact on Music |
|------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Economic Recession | High (deferred projects) | Low (mandatory need) | Medium (discretionary) |
| Regulatory Change | Medium (WCAG updates) | High (ACA changes) | Low |
| Competition | High (low barriers) | Medium (local) | Low (niche) |
| Technology Shift | Medium (AI evolution) | Low | Medium (platforms) |

### Contingency Plans
1. **WCAG Slowdown**: Shift focus to insurance sales (faster close)
2. **Insurance Rule Changes**: Focus on Medicare (stable)
3. **Music Platform Failure**: Pivot to consulting/scoring only
4. **AI Cost Spike**: Raise WCAG prices or reduce AI usage
