# Day One Implementation Budget
## WCAG AI Platform - Budget-Friendly Startup Guide

**Target Audience**: Bootstrapped entrepreneurs starting with $5,000-$10,000 in capital

---

## Executive Summary

This guide provides a realistic, budget-conscious path to launching the WCAG AI Platform within 30 days with minimal upfront capital. Focus is on revenue-generating activities while deferring non-critical expenses.

**Total Estimated Cost**: $6,200-$8,500 (first 30 days)  
**Break-Even Target**: 1-2 client projects ($7,500-$15,000)  
**Timeline**: 4 weeks to first paying client

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-7) - $1,800-$2,500

### Essential Legal ($125-$800)
- [ ] **Business Formation**: S-Corp via Stripe Atlas ($500) OR DIY LLC ($125-$300)
  - **Recommendation**: Start with LLC for speed, convert to S-Corp at $50K revenue
  - **Timeline**: 5-7 business days
  - **Priority**: HIGH (liability protection)

- [ ] **EIN Registration**: Free from IRS
  - **Timeline**: Same day online
  - **Priority**: HIGH (required for bank account)

- [ ] **Business Bank Account**: $0-$25 monthly fee
  - **Recommendation**: Mercury, Novo, or local credit union
  - **Priority**: HIGH (separate personal/business finances)

### Deferred Legal Expenses (First 90 Days)
- ~~Professional Liability Insurance~~: Start after first client signed ($3,000/year)
- ~~Attorney Review~~: Use templates until $25K revenue ($300-$500)
- ~~Contracts~~: Use provided templates with AI disclosure

### Essential Tools ($200-$300/month)
- [ ] **Development Tools** ($50/month):
  - Railway (backend hosting): $20/month
  - Vercel (frontend hosting): Free tier initially
  - GitHub (version control): Free
  
- [ ] **Business Communication** ($50/month):
  - Google Workspace OR Microsoft 365: $6-$12/user
  - Professional email domain: $12/year (Namecheap)
  - Phone: Google Voice (free) OR Grasshopper ($29/month)
  
- [ ] **CRM & Sales** ($50/month):
  - HubSpot Free tier OR Zoho CRM: $0-$50/month
  - LinkedIn Basic: Free (upgrade to Sales Nav after first client)
  
- [ ] **AI Tools** ($50/month initially):
  - Anthropic Claude API: Pay-as-you-go ($0.50-$3.00 per audit)
  - OpenAI API (backup): Pay-as-you-go
  - **Budget**: $50/month allows 15-25 AI-assisted audits

### One-Time Setup ($500-$700)
- [ ] **Domain & Branding** ($100-$200):
  - Domain name: $12-$50/year
  - Logo design: Canva Pro ($15/month) or Fiverr ($50-$100)
  - Business cards: Vistaprint ($30-$50)
  
- [ ] **Website/Landing Page** ($0-$300):
  - Option A: WordPress + Elementor (free)
  - Option B: Webflow free tier
  - Option C: Custom React site (your platform as demo)
  
- [ ] **Accounting Software** ($15-$30/month):
  - Wave (free for basic) OR QuickBooks Simple Start ($30/month)
  
- [ ] **Project Management** ($0):
  - Notion (free tier) OR Trello (free tier)

### Learning & Certification ($200-$700)
- [ ] **WCAG Certification** (Optional Year 1):
  - IAAP CPACC: $395 (exam only)
  - Deloitte Digital Accessibility: Free course
  - **Recommendation**: Start with free courses, get CPACC after 5 projects
  
- [ ] **Technical Skills**:
  - Udemy WCAG course: $15-$30 (during sale)
  - YouTube tutorials: Free
  - WCAG 2.1 documentation: Free

## Cash Flow Management

### Payment Terms Strategy
- **50% upfront**: Required for new clients
- **50% on delivery**: Within 7 days of report delivery
- **Net 30**: Only for established clients

### Emergency Fund
- Maintain 3 months operating expenses ($3,600-$5,000)
- Separate business savings account
- Fund from first 3 client payments

### Quarterly Tax Estimates
- Set aside 30% of profit for taxes
- Make quarterly estimated payments
- Work with accountant or use software

## Mistakes to Avoid

### Don't Spend Money On
1. ❌ Expensive office space (work from home/coffee shops)
2. ❌ Logo designers before first client
3. ❌ Multiple software subscriptions (pick one per category)
4. ❌ Premium tools you won't use daily
5. ❌ Advertising before validating messaging
6. ❌ Business coach/consultant (learn by doing)
7. ❌ Fancy business cards/swag

### Do Spend Money On
1. ✅ Professional liability insurance (after first client)
2. ✅ Quality testing tools (drive revenue)
3. ✅ Lead generation (LinkedIn, email)
4. ✅ Certifications (CPACC increases credibility)
5. ✅ Accounting/legal (prevent costly mistakes)
6. ✅ Website/portfolio (showcase expertise)
