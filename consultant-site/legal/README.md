# Legal Documents Generator

This directory contains templates for auto-generating legal documents for your WCAG consulting service.

## Important Notice

**⚠️ LEGAL DISCLAIMER:** These templates are starting points only. You MUST have them reviewed by a qualified attorney before using them in production. Legal requirements vary by jurisdiction.

## Quick Setup

1. Have attorney review and customize templates
2. Save final versions to `consultant-site/public/legal/`
3. Link from footer: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy
4. Require acceptance during onboarding
5. Store acceptance records for audit trail

## Key Legal Requirements

### Terms of Service Must Include:
- Service description and limitations
- No warranty on ADA compliance
- Client responsibilities
- Liability limitations
- Subscription and billing terms
- Cancellation policy
- Dispute resolution

### Privacy Policy Must Include:
- Data collection practices
- Data usage and sharing
- User rights (access, deletion, portability)
- GDPR compliance (EU users)
- CCPA compliance (California users)
- Cookie policy
- Data retention periods

## Contact Attorney

Before launching your consulting service, consult with:

1. **Business Attorney** - Review Terms of Service
2. **Privacy Attorney** - Review Privacy Policy
3. **Insurance Broker** - Professional liability insurance

Typical cost: $2,000-$5,000 for initial legal review
