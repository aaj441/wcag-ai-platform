## üéØ Priority Scoring System

**Score each prospect 1-10 on:**

1. **Website Accessibility** (1-10)
   - 1 = Perfectly compliant (skip)
   - 10 = Obviously broken (high priority)

2. **Company Size** (1-10)
   - Small (1-3): $10K-$20K deal
   - Medium (4-7): $20K-$50K deal
   - Large (8-10): $50K-$150K deal

3. **Recent Lawsuits** (1-10)
   - Check: https://www.adatitleiii.com
   - 1 = No lawsuits
   - 10 = Multiple recent lawsuits (HOT LEAD!)

4. **Digital Presence** (1-10)
   - 1 = Simple website only
   - 10 = Complex app + portal + mobile (more violations = bigger deal)

**Priority Formula**: (Accessibility Score √ó 2) + Company Size + Recent Lawsuits + Digital Presence

**Target: Score 25+** = High priority, contact immediately

## üîç Quick Scanning Workflow

For each company:
1. **Find website**: Google "[Company Name] + login" or "patient portal"
2. **Run WAVE scan**: https://wave.webaim.org/
3. **Screenshot violations**: Save for email
4. **Find decision-maker**: LinkedIn search "CTO" or "VP Engineering" or "Compliance Officer"
5. **Get email**: hunter.io or RocketReach
6. **Send personalized email**: Use template + specific violations found

## üìä Target Metrics

**Monthly Goals:**
- **Scans**: 300 companies (100 per industry)
- **Emails Sent**: 300 personalized emails
- **Responses**: 9-15 (3-5% response rate)
- **Demos**: 5-7 (30-50% of responses)
- **Closed Deals**: 2-3 (30-40% of demos)
- **Revenue**: $40K-$120K MRR

**Weekly Breakdown:**
- Week 1: Scan 75 companies, send 75 emails
- Week 2: Scan 75 companies, send 75 emails, follow up Week 1
- Week 3: Scan 75 companies, send 75 emails, follow up Week 2
- Week 4: Scan 75 companies, send 75 emails, close deals
