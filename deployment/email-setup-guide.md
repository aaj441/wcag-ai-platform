## Email Warming Strategy

**CRITICAL**: Don't send 100+ emails immediately from new account!

**Week 1**: Send 5-10 emails/day
**Week 2**: Send 10-20 emails/day
**Week 3**: Send 20-40 emails/day
**Week 4+**: Full volume (50-100/day)

**Warming Actions:**
- Send emails to yourself
- Have conversations with friends/colleagues
- Join mailing lists and receive emails
- Don't use spam trigger words initially

## Spam Avoidance Checklist

‚úÖ Personalize each email (use company name)
‚úÖ Include unsubscribe link
‚úÖ Don't use ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation!!!
‚úÖ Avoid spam trigger words: FREE, GUARANTEED, ACT NOW
‚úÖ Keep emails under 200 words initially
‚úÖ Include your physical address (can be home office)
‚úÖ Add SPF, DKIM records (if custom domain)
