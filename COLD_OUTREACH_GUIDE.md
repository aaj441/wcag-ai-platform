### A/B Testing Framework

#### Test Variables

1. **Subject Lines**
   - Urgency vs. Curiosity
   - Violation count vs. Risk level
   - Personal vs. Company name

2. **Email Length**
   - 3 sentences (50-75 words)
   - 5 sentences (100-125 words)
   - Full paragraph (150-200 words)

3. **Call-to-Action**
   - "Reply YES" vs. "Schedule call"
   - Specific time slots vs. Open calendar link
   - Question vs. Statement

4. **Hooks**
   - Lawsuit risk
   - Revenue loss
   - Competitive advantage
   - Value-first (free audit)
