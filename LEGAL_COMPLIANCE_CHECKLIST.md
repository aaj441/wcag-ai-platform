## Pre-Launch Legal Checklist

## Emergency Compliance Procedures

### If Served with Legal Documents
1. **Immediately**: Contact attorney (within 24 hours)
2. **Do Not**: Ignore or respond without legal counsel
3. **Do**: Preserve all relevant documents and communications
4. **Notify**: Insurance carrier if related to professional services
5. **Document**: All actions taken and timeline

### If Data Breach Occurs
1. **Hour 1**: Contain breach, isolate affected systems
2. **Hour 2**: Notify compliance officer and legal counsel
3. **Hour 4**: Assess scope of breach
4. **Hour 24**: Notify cyber insurance carrier
5. **Hour 72**: Notify affected clients (if required by law)
6. **Week 1**: Implement corrective measures
7. **Week 2**: File required regulatory notifications

### If Client Complaint Received
1. **Day 1**: Acknowledge receipt in writing
2. **Day 2**: Review contract and project documentation
3. **Day 3**: Consult with attorney if liability concern
4. **Day 5**: Notify insurance carrier (if potential claim)
5. **Day 7**: Respond to client with proposed resolution
6. **Ongoing**: Document all communications

## Red Flags: When to Consult Attorney Immediately

### Legal Warning Signs
- ⚠️ Received demand letter or cease & desist
- ⚠️ Threatened with lawsuit
- ⚠️ Client alleging professional negligence
- ⚠️ Regulatory investigation or inquiry
- ⚠️ Contract dispute escalating
- ⚠️ Employee/contractor claiming misclassification
- ⚠️ Intellectual property infringement claim
- ⚠️ Data breach affecting client data
- ⚠️ Insurance claim denial
- ⚠️ Significant change in business structure/ownership

### Do NOT:
- ❌ Admit fault or liability
- ❌ Negotiate without attorney guidance
- ❌ Destroy any documents
- ❌ Discuss publicly or on social media
- ❌ Ignore deadlines in legal documents
