## 8. KEY RISKS & MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### Risk 1: Legal Liability for "Guaranteed Compliance"
**Problem:** We guarantee compliance but client still gets sued
**Probability:** LOW (but high impact)
**Mitigation:**
- E&O insurance ($2M policy - $3K/year)
- Service agreement includes liability limitations
- Partner with accessibility lawyers for 2nd opinions
- Offer optional legal defense fund ($50/mo add-on)
- Document all remediation work (audit trail)

### Risk 2: Technology Obsolescence
**Problem:** WCAG 3.0 or new standards make tools outdated
**Probability:** MEDIUM (happens every 5-10 years)
**Mitigation:**
- Modular architecture (easy to swap components)
- Subscribe to WCAG working group changes
- Regular updates to scanning engine (monthly)
- Keep AI models current (quarterly)

### Risk 3: Customer Acquisition Cost Too High
**Problem:** CAC exceeds $50 (breaks unit economics)
**Probability:** MEDIUM
**Mitigation:**
- Automate everything (reduce manual work)
- Free audit lead magnet (low-cost acquisition)
- Referral program (20% discount for each referral)
- Content marketing (organic traffic reduces CAC)
- Niche targeting (higher conversion = lower CAC)

### Risk 4: High Churn Rate
**Problem:** Clients cancel after initial fix
**Probability:** MEDIUM
**Mitigation:**
- Show ongoing value (monthly compliance reports)
- New regulations = new violations (they need us)
- Annual contracts with 10% discount (lock-in)
- Customer success team (proactive outreach)
- NPS tracking + quarterly reviews

### Risk 5: Market Saturation
**Problem:** Competitors enter and undercut pricing
**Probability:** MEDIUM (everyone sees the opportunity)
**Mitigation:**
