## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Scope of Services
Provider agrees to perform web accessibility consulting services as described in individual Statements of Work (SOW) executed under this Agreement, including but not limited to:

- Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1/2.2 compliance assessments
- Accessibility audit reports and documentation
- Remediation recommendations and implementation guidance
- Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) documentation
- Section 508 compliance analysis
- ADA Title III digital accessibility consulting

### 1.2 AI-Assisted Analysis

**CRITICAL DISCLOSURE**: Provider utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning tools to assist in accessibility analysis and violation detection. Client acknowledges and agrees that:

1. **AI Augmentation**: AI tools are used to enhance efficiency and coverage of accessibility assessments
2. **Human Oversight**: All AI-generated findings are reviewed by qualified human accessibility consultants
3. **No Autonomous Decisions**: No recommendations or reports are issued without professional human review
4. **Confidence Scoring**: AI findings include confidence scores indicating reliability of automated detection
5. **Professional Judgment**: Final determinations are made by certified accessibility professionals

### 1.3 Deliverables
Unless otherwise specified in individual SOWs, deliverables may include:
- Accessibility audit reports
- VPAT documentation (Section 508 or WCAG 2.x)
- Prioritized remediation roadmap
- Code-level recommendations
- Executive summary presentations
