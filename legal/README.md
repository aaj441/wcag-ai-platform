# Legal Foundation - WCAG AI Platform

## Overview
This directory contains legal documentation, compliance requirements, and contractual templates for the WCAG AI Platform business operations.

## Directory Structure

### `/insurance/`
Insurance requirements, policies, and compliance documentation for:
- Technology Errors & Omissions (E&O) Insurance
- Cyber Liability Insurance
- General Liability Insurance
- Professional Liability Coverage

### `/contracts/`
Client contract templates and legal agreements:
- Master Service Agreements (MSA)
- Statement of Work (SOW) templates
- AI Disclosure & Liability documents
- Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA)
- Terms of Service (ToS)

### `/compliance/`
Regulatory compliance documentation:
- WCAG compliance audit trails
- HIPAA compliance (for health insurance features)
- GDPR/CCPA data privacy compliance
- ADA consulting liability disclaimers
- Audit logging requirements

## Critical Legal Requirements

### 1. Business Formation
- **Entity Type**: S-Corporation (recommended)
- **Cost**: $125-$800 (Stripe Atlas: $500)
- **Timeline**: 1-2 weeks
- **Benefits**: Limited liability, tax advantages, professional credibility

### 2. Insurance Coverage (Annual Costs)
| Coverage Type | Annual Cost | Coverage Limit |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| Tech E&O | $2,500-$4,000 | $1M-$2M |
| Cyber Liability | $1,500-$2,500 | $1M |
| General Liability | $1,200-$1,500 | $1M/$2M |
| Professional Liability | $600-$1,200 | $500K-$1M |
| **Total** | **$5,800-$9,200** | - |

### 3. Legal Review Budget
- Initial contract review: $300-$500
- Ongoing legal consultation: $200-$400/month
- Annual compliance audit: $1,000-$2,000

## AI Disclosure Requirements

All client contracts MUST include:
1. **AI-Assisted Analysis Disclosure**: Clear statement that AI tools are used
2. **Human-in-the-Loop Guarantee**: Professional review of all AI outputs
3. **Liability Limitations**: Scope of responsibility for AI recommendations
4. **Data Privacy**: How client data is processed and stored
5. **Accuracy Disclaimers**: No warranty on 100% accuracy claims

## Compliance Logging

All platform activities must maintain audit trails for:
- Client communications (email drafts, approvals)
- WCAG scan results and recommendations
- Human consultant reviews and overrides
- AI confidence scores and reasoning
- Data processing and storage events
